Doctor

A doctor is a medical professional who diagnoses and treats illnesses and injuries in patients. They conduct physical examinations, order and interpret diagnostic tests, and develop treatment plans. Doctors also provide preventive care, educate patients about health and wellness, and may work in various settings including hospitals, clinics, and private practices.

Teacher

A teacher is an educational professional who imparts knowledge and skills to students at various levels. Teachers create a supportive learning environment, develop and implement lesson plans, and assess student progress to foster academic and personal growth.

Helper

A helper provides support and assistance in various tasks, often in non-medical settings. The role can vary widely depending on the specific job or industry, but generally involves assisting others with their daily tasks and contributing to the smooth operation of activities.
